Gilmore Girls

Set in a storybook Connecticut town populated by an eclectic mix of dreamers, artists and everyday folk, this multigenerational drama about family and friendship centers around Lorelai Gilmore and her daughter, Rory. Lorelai owns the Dragonfly Inn, with best friend/chef Sookie, and contends with eccentric, well-off parents Richard and Emily Gilmore.
