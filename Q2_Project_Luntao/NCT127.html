NCT 127 is NCT's Seoul team and consists of 10 members, Taeyong, Taeil, Yuta, Jaehyun, Winwin, Mark, Haechan, Doyoung, Johnny, and Jungwoo, who have various nationalities such as Korea, China, Japan, and Canada. The number 127 in the team name means the longitude of Seoul, which means that the group will be active on the global stage based on Seoul, the home of K-POP.
